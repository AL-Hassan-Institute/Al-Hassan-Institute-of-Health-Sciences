Diploma in Nursing

Qualification: Diploma in Nursing
Duration: 3 Years
Study Mode: Full-time
Award: Recognized Professional Qualification

Program Overview

This program provides comprehensive training in nursing practice, combining theory and practical clinical experience. Students will develop skills in patient care, community health, and professional ethics to meet the healthcare needs of Uganda and the wider region.

Career Opportunities

  • Registered Nurse in hospitals and clinics
  • Community Health Officer
  • Public Health Specialist
  • NGO Health Program Coordinator

Entry Requirements

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) or equivalent
  • Passes in Biology and Chemistry
  • Demonstrated commitment to healthcare service

Course Content

  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Medical and Surgical Nursing
  • Community Health Nursing
  • Pharmacology and Therapeutics
  • Clinical Practice and Patient Care
  • Ethics and Professionalism
