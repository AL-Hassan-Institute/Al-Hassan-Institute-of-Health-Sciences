Certificate in Pharmacy

Qualification: Certificate in Pharmacy
Duration: 1 Year
Study Mode: Full-time
Award: Recognized Professional Qualification

Program Overview

This program equips students with foundational knowledge and skills in pharmacy practice. Graduates will assist pharmacists in dispensing medication, providing patient counseling, and supporting pharmaceutical services in hospitals, clinics, and community pharmacies.

Career Opportunities

  • Pharmacy Assistant
  • Dispensing Aide in hospitals or clinics
  • Community Pharmacy Support
  • NGO or Government Health Projects

Entry Requirements

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) or equivalent
  • Passes in Biology and Chemistry
  • Interest in pharmacy and healthcare services

Course Content

  • Pharmacy Fundamentals
  • Dispensing & Prescription Practices
  • Pharmacology Basics
  • Drug Safety and Storage
  • Patient Counseling & Communication
  • Community Pharmacy Practice
  • Practical Attachments / Hospital Placement
