Admission Requirements

Eligibility criteria for all academic programs

Important Notice:
Admission is competitive and based on academic merit, availability of space, and verification of qualifications.

Diploma Programs Requirements

  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) or equivalent
  • At least one principal pass in Biology or Chemistry
  • Minimum of 5 passes at UCE including English, Biology, and Mathematics
  • Applicant must be medically fit
  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Must pass institutional screening and interview

Certificate Programs Requirements

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE)
  • Minimum passes in English, Biology, and one science subject
  • Good character certificate
  • Recommendation letter from former school

International Students Requirements

  • Equivalent academic qualifications
  • Certified academic transcripts
  • English proficiency certificate
  • Valid passport

Required Documents Checklist

  • Certified academic certificates
  • Academic transcripts
  • National ID or passport copy
  • Birth certificate
  • 4 passport size photographs
  • Application fee receipt
