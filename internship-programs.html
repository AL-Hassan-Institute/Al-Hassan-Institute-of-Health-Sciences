Internship Programs

Our internship programs provide students with real-world professional experience in healthcare, laboratories, research, and community outreach. These programs prepare students for employment and professional excellence.

Hospital Internship

Gain practical clinical experience working with healthcare professionals in hospitals and medical centers.

  • Patient care experience
  • Clinical observation
  • Hospital procedures
  • Professional mentorship

Laboratory Internship

Develop laboratory diagnostic skills using modern equipment and professional techniques.

  • Sample testing
  • Laboratory procedures
  • Equipment handling
  • Quality assurance

Community Health Internship

Participate in public health outreach programs and community healthcare initiatives.

  • Community outreach
  • Health education
  • Disease prevention
  • Public health support

Research Internship

Support institutional research projects and develop academic and analytical skills.

  • Data collection
  • Research methods
  • Academic writing
  • Project participation
